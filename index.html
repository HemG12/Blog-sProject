Indori Poha

A Lightweight Breakfast For your Heavy Workload Indori Poha

  1. Take flattened rice (poha) and rinse it under cold water.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds, cumin seeds, and curry leaves.
  3. Add chopped onions, green chilies, and ginger; sauté until onions are translucent.
  4. Add the rinsed poha, turmeric powder, and salt; mix well.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with chutney.
  6. Enjoy your meal!
  7. Wash the dishes don't be the dummy
